Sugar is now cheaper, making life sweeter

Price control measures, like price ceilings work. They do not create shortages, they do not distort the price mechanism and they are good incentives for consumers to to use those goods sparingly. They also create extra allocative efficiency. Plus, supermarkets would never dare to make up for their losses by increasing the price of other products.
